Our Green Mandate

Closed-Loop Manufacturing

We have pioneered a proprietary water reclamation system that allows our mills to recycle 95% of the water used in the pulping process. By keeping our systems closed, we protect local watersheds and ensure that our environmental footprint remains minimal.

Our goal is to reach 100% carbon neutrality by 2030 through the integration of solar arrays and biomass energy at our primary facilities.

100% Certified

All raw materials are FSC certified, ensuring they come from responsibly managed forests.

Zero Landfill

Our production scraps are repurposed into industrial packaging, leaving nothing to waste.

Bio-Powered

We utilize wood byproducts to generate clean steam energy for our drying rollers.

Our Story

From a small family-owned mill to a global pioneer in sustainable fiber technologies.

[Image of paper manufacturing process]

The Matalick Way

At Matalick Inc., we believe paper is more than just a surface for ink. It is a vital tool for communication, an essential part of commerce, and a renewable resource that must be managed with absolute care.

Founded on the principle of "Quality without Compromise," we have spent three decades perfecting the balance between high-performance paper characteristics and environmentally responsible production.

Milestones of Excellence

1994

The First Roll

Matalick Inc. begins operations in a renovated timber mill, focusing on high-durability blueprint paper.

2005

Sustainability First

We launched our EverGreen line, becoming one of the first regional mills to use 100% post-consumer recycled fiber.

2018

Global Reach

Expansion of our distribution network to three continents, serving over 500 premium brands worldwide.

2026

Digital Integration

Leading the way in "Smart Paper" solutions that bridge the gap between physical touch and digital data.
